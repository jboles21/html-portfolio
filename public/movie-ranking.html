The Best Movies According to Joshua

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

If you can see the pattern of my top movie choices, my love of movies are in connection to my love for manipulating reality itself. And inception is a great example of the reality bending posibilities that are capable of occuring once you understand the power of your mind.

Memento

Now, this movie does not typically fall into the same category of the others, but if you look at the movie from a directing POV then it still has that reality bending perspective. I wont ruin it for you just go watch all 3.
